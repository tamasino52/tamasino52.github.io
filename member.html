Welcome to the SSU Computer Vision Laboratory

We researched and developed various topics in the field of computer vision under supervision of Prof. Gye-young Kim. We mainly focuses on having artificial intelligence close to humans by extracting, reconstructing, and recognizing information from digital images. Recently, our main areas of interest are deep learning, 3D human pose estimation, anomaly detection, and high-speed image analysis.
